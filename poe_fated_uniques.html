This sheet is only useful until you can run T2 maps reliably and have ~150c in the bank.
At that point map-running profits are far faster than trying to purchase/run prophecies.
You can compile 6-12 unidentified rare sets (or more) worth 2c each in the time it takes to sell even one of these items, that's 12c-24c right there.
(Buy rings/amulets/belts for 1alc each in bulk, 12-24 at a time.)

This calculator assumes 1c = 1c, 1 exa = 100c, and everything else = .1c
I know it's not exact, but it's close enough for you to see what makes sense to craft without having to do a shitload more coding. It's not worth the extra effort on the backend.

This calculator is going by the lowest price for each item.
Be aware the resulting item may sell for even more than shown here, depending on the stat rolls your item gets or how many sockets you can put on it.

Tip: Don't look at this tool expecting to buy from scratch. A better use of this tool is to check your drops and get familiar with these items.
This is faster because you get the item running maps (which means you're leveling) instead of messaging 20 people trying to buy it (which means you're not leveling).
When you get a unique drop check here to see if it's worth upgrading. Once you've upgraded it use the trade links here to check prices if you don't have the Poe Trade Macro installed.

If you're flat broke and need to make some currency then PLEASE - DO NOT MAKE MULTIPLES OF THE SAME ITEM !!!
That makes everyone's prices go down, including yours.
Either pick 2-3 items and only make one of each or "go all in" and invest all your spare currency on one expensive item.
Just because there is a POTENTIAL profit doesn't mean anything if your item doesn't sell. Check poe.ninja to check interest in the item.
